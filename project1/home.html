Balancing work and parenting is a challenge that many working parents face today. At Small Steps DayCare, we understand this need and offer a state-of-the-art Day Care Facility in Delhi and Gurugram. Children are divided into age appropriate batches which ensures suitable mentoring towards their social, emotional, physical and other areas of development. Music, Dance, Physical Education, Art Enrichment and Drama are some of the highlights of the program.

Extended Day Care: Parents of our pre-schoolers may additionally opt for the Extended Day Care program for their children. Under this program, children attend the structured preschool program in the morning hours and are catered to specialized programs by our day care and specialist teachers after pre-school hours. Meals and adequate rest is provided to the children throughout the day.

Afterschool Day Care: Children who are beyond preschool age may continue to use our Day Care Facility by enrolling in the Afternoon Day Care program. This program is also open to children from other preschools and primary schools. Children may be dropped off to any of our day care centres post their school hours. After consuming meals and getting adequate rest, children are engaged in structured activities by our day care and specialist teachers.
